Did You Know?

Learn how small actions create big changes

Water Conservation

Reducing your shower time by just 2 minutes can save up to 10 gallons of water per day. That's 3,650 gallons per year!

Plastic Reduction

A single reusable bag can eliminate the need for over 1,000 plastic bags in its lifetime, preventing tons of plastic waste.

Energy Efficiency

Unplugging electronics when not in use can reduce your energy bill by up to 10% and prevent unnecessary carbon emissions.
